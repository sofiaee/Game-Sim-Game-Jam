Rock Stingers Pack
https://www.assetstore.unity3d.com/en/#!/content/38882


This pack contains 25 short clips in rock style, a few seconds long, to be used for intros, endings, deaths, victories to emphasize particular moments in games. 

 You can listen to the demo of the whole package: https://soundcloud.com/bosone/rock-logo-stingers-demo-for-unity3d-asset-store

 This is the initial release of the asset. It will be expanded with more clips and it will part of a more extended project called "rock loops construction kit", that will be sold separately. 

 If you purchase this initial release you will encourage me in the development, you will be able to get the future updates of this asset free of charge and to upgrade to the complete "rock loops construction kit" at a discounted price. 

 Future updates of this asset will also include some bonus content.
